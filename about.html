About Professor TP

Professor TP is a novel writer who joined the writing indusrty in 2016 with the novel Kuhovhiyo Tenda Wadzoka which became a trending novel across Southern Africa in that year. Professor's real name is Tichaona Paul who was born in June 14th 1997 in the province of Goromonzi and grew up in rural areas.

Someone may want to know what motivated the legendary writer of action novels and realistic based bublical and evil human thoughts to to expose it in writings? The answer is here, sometimes you might not know what is your purpose on this earth if God does not reveal it in you, but to your author He does!
